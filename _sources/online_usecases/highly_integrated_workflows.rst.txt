###########################
Highly Integrated Workflows
###########################

The Highly Integrated Workflows collect the Products resulting from the
Co-Design Project 2 (CDP2), which focuses on "Mouse-based cellular cortical and subcortical microcircuit models" and aims at integrating the activity of the HBP Platforms in order to refine brain modelling tools.
